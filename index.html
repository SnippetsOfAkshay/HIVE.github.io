Why waste when you can donate?

HIVE is the first mobile app on the market to harness the power of community that cares. This app provides an unique opportunity to share your resources with anyone in your area.

  • Users can create profiles with essential information and authenticate their identity for community trust and engagement.
  • Users can list items they want to donate or find items they need through a comprehensive search and filter system.
  • The app facilitates seamless communication between donors and recipients and allows for scheduling pick-ups and deliveries, enhancing the donation process.
  • Safety measures ensure secure transactions, including item condition verification, and a review system helps build credibility within the community.
